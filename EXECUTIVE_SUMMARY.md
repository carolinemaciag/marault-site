## New Conversation Flows

### Flow 1: Skeptical Buyer (Most Important)
```
User: "This sounds vague. What do you actually deliver?"
Bot: "Fair question. We deliver tangible outputs: dashboards, 
     forecasting models, websites, UX systems. The real value 
     is how those outputs improve decisions."

User: "Why not just use a cheaper agency?"
Bot: "Surface-level execution vs structured thinking. We focus 
     on clarity, structure, and long-term usability. Cheaper 
     often means rebuilding later."

User: "You're newer—why take you seriously?"
Bot: "Seriousness comes from quality of thinking and clarity 
     of execution, not age. We deliver work that holds up 
     under scrutiny."
```

### Flow 2: Executive Decision-Maker
```
User: "How do you measure success?"
Bot: "Improved decision speed, clearer understanding of drivers,
     stronger conversion, reduced confusion."

User: "Why not hire internally?"
Bot: "Internal teams are often too close, lack frameworks, focus 
     on execution. We bring outside perspective, structured thinking,
     focus on decision quality."

User: "What's your philosophy on design and analytics?"
Bot: "Both should serve one goal: reduce uncertainty and increase
     confidence in decisions. Not decoration or dashboards for sake
     of dashboards."
```

### Flow 3: New Prospect (Awareness)
```
User: "What does your company do?"
Bot: "We help businesses make clearer, higher-quality decisions.
     Includes understanding revenue drivers, building forecasting 
     systems, improving websites, designing better interfaces."

User: "What makes you different?"
Bot: "Most focus on visuals OR data. We do both. More importantly,
     we focus on decision clarity: what's happening, why, what to do."

User: "How do I know if I need this?"
Bot: "If you've thought 'this should be clearer,' 'we're guessing 
     too much,' then you're likely a fit."
```
