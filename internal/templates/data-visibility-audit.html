Data Visibility Audit

Fix messy data and give owners real visibility. We define what matters, clean and consolidate your data, and deliver one executive dashboard with a clear insight summary.

What we do

Many growing businesses accumulate data across platforms — accounting software, CRM systems, e-commerce tools, spreadsheets, and reporting dashboards — without a unified view of performance.

We begin by defining what actually matters. Not every metric deserves attention. We identify the few indicators that drive operational decisions.

We then clean, standardize, and consolidate your data into a structured foundation. From there, we build one executive-level dashboard designed for scanning — not interpretation.

Finally, we provide a written insight summary outlining what to watch, what’s working, and where attention is required.

Outcome

Executives should not hesitate before making decisions because they are unsure whether the data is reliable.

“I trust my numbers.”

That clarity changes how leadership operates — meetings become focused, priorities sharpen, and decisions move faster.

Why it’s critical

This engagement is intentionally designed as a low-friction entry point. It provides immediate clarity while laying the groundwork for deeper analytics.

  • Low-friction starting point for leadership alignment
  • Opens the door to forecasting, modeling, and advanced BI
  • Builds trust quickly through clarity and structure

Why Marault Intelligence?

We build disciplined systems that leadership can trust. The goal is clarity, structure, and long-term confidence — not just another dashboard.
